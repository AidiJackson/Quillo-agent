# Quillo Pricing Ladder

## Philosophy
**Start with self-service tools (DIY Suite), upgrade to full orchestration (Quillo Team)**

Key insight: DIY Suite users learn the value of individual tools, then upgrade to Quillo Team for intelligent orchestration that bundles everything with AI judgment.

**For individuals exploring AI-assisted communication**

- Basic response generator
- Simple rewriter
- Access to public templates
- Community support

**Price**: Free forever

## FAQs

**Q: Can I mix and match tools?**
A: Yes! DIY Suite users can use tools individually. Quillo Team includes everything.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my request limit?**
A: You'll be notified at 80% usage. You can upgrade or purchase extra requests.

**Q: Can I downgrade anytime?**
A: Yes, downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
A: Yes! 14-day free trial on Quillo Team Starter (no credit card required).

**Q: Do you offer student/nonprofit discounts?**
A: Yes! 40% off DIY Suite and Quillo Team plans. Contact us for verification.
